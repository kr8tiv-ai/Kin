# Voice Features Guide

Kin companions support voice interaction through the voice loop feature, enabling natural spoken conversations.

## Voice Loop Overview

Voice loop creates a continuous conversation flow:
1. You speak to your Kin
2. Kin processes and responds with voice
3. Conversation continues naturally

## Enabling Voice Features

### Prerequisites
- Microphone access granted
- Speaker/audio output available
- Voice loop enabled in Kin settings

### Setup Steps
1. Open Mission Control
2. Navigate to your Kin's settings
3. Enable "Voice Loop" toggle
4. Grant microphone permissions when prompted
5. Test with a short phrase

## Voice Commands

### General Commands
- "Hey [Kin name], help" - Show available commands
- "Stop listening" - Pause voice loop
- "Continue" - Resume voice loop

### Quick Actions
- "Check my VPS health"
- "Show network status"
- "Open settings"

## Troubleshooting

### Microphone Not Working
- Check browser permissions (Settings > Privacy > Microphone)
- Verify microphone is not muted
- Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)

### Voice Not Clear
- Check speaker volume
- Reduce background noise
- Use headphones for better quality

### Delayed Responses
- Check internet connection
- Reduce audio quality in settings
- Close other audio applications

### Voice Loop Stops
- Say "Continue" to resume
- Check if battery saver is active
- Verify Kin is still responsive

## Privacy

- Voice recordings are only stored if consented
- Recordings can be deleted anytime
- Voice data is encrypted in transit
- Local processing preferred when possible

## Best Practices

1. Use a quality microphone for better recognition
2. Speak clearly at normal pace
3. Minimize background noise
4. Use headphones for private conversations
