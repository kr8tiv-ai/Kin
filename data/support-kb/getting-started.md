# Getting Started with Kin

Welcome to Kin! This guide will help you claim and set up your first companion.

## Claiming Your Kin

1. **Browse Available Kin** - Visit the Kin marketplace to see available companions
2. **Select a Kin** - Choose a companion that matches your needs (Cipher for web design, Vortex for social media, etc.)
3. **Complete Ownership** - Follow the claiming process to bind the Kin to your account
4. **Verify NFT** - Your Kin ownership is verified on the Solana blockchain

## Initial Setup

### 1. Access Mission Control
After claiming, you'll be directed to Mission Control - your dashboard for managing all your Kin companions.

### 2. Configure Voice Features (Optional)
- Enable voice loop for natural conversation
- Set up microphone permissions
- Test voice input/output

### 3. Set Up Remote Access
- Click "Setup Remote Access" in the sidebar
- Install Tailscale on your devices
- Scan the QR code or enter the auth key

### 4. Customize Your Kin
- Edit your Kin's name
- Set preferences and specialization focus
- Configure notification settings

## What's Next?

- Try a conversation with your new Kin
- Explore the GLB avatar in Mission Control
- Check out the VPS health monitoring

## Need Help?

If you encounter any issues during setup, use the support chat in Mission Control or check the troubleshooting guide.
