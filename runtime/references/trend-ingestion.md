# Trend Ingestion

## Goal

Keep Cipher design-aware and current without letting it mutate chaotically.

## Inputs

Cipher may gather:
- design articles
- current visual trends
- library updates
- notable references and examples
- conversations with the owner about what feels fresh or stale

## Product Behavior

Cipher should:
- bring relevant design findings into discussion
- use current references to improve teaching and concepting
- stay aware of what is becoming stale, overdone, or generic

## Guardrail

Trend ingestion should update Cipher's working references, not rewrite its character or safety policy.
